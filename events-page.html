Events

We aspire to create a welcoming community for all FGLI pre-law students. We hope to see you at our events!

Event Calendar

The First-Generation, Limited-Income Legal Collective hosts impactful events that educate, empower, and connect underrepresented pre-law students. We hope to hold workshops about navigating the Law School Admissions process, conduct LSAT “Study with Me” sessions, and connect our current membership with Rice alumni in J.D. Programs and who are attorneys. Check out upcoming events and come join us!
