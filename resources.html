Resources

This resource page is intended to help reduce the confusion about the Law School Application process. Please consider this database a starting point and make sure to look at other resource databases.

Recommended Prelaw Timeline

Freshman

Build a strong academic foundation and explore interests through pre-law organizations and activities.

Sophomore

Gain relevant experience through internships, advocacy, or leadership roles, identify areas of interest in law.

Junior Fall

Begin studying for the LSAT and researching potential Law schools to create a preliminary list.

Junior Spring

Take the LSAT, start networking with admissions officers, and prepare application materials like personal statements and résumés.

Senior

Apply to Law school early in the cycle, following up with schools and attending interviews as needed!
